Our Mission & Vision

Why We Started This Project

Anēk (अनेक) means 'many'. Basically, one person ('Ek') doing something cool inspires a bunch of other people ('Anēk').

We're tired of waiting on others. We built this so you can take charge of your street, report what's broken, fix it with your neighbors, and actually get rewarded for it.
